    Application Criteria And Instructions

    Instruction

    1. FireHack will use your primary contact's email address for all follow-up correspondence.
    2. Please do NOT submit draft of your application until you are ready to submit for final consideration.One team can submit only one application.
    3. We review applications on a rolling basis, so it's to your advantage to submit as soon as you are ready.
    4. Applications for FireHack Mumbai are due by Sunday, July 20, 2014 at 11:59pm IST.
    5. You will receive a confirmation page once you submit your application.
    6. Required fields are marked with a *.
    7. If your team has been selected, you will receive acknowledgement by July 30th. Along with acknowledgement, ticket/passes payment details will be provided.
    8. Your team has to confirm participation within 48hrs since Acknowledgement. Failing to do so will result in discarding your entry.

    Criteria:

    1. FireHack is open to all types of hardware devices + mobile apps.
    2. "In selecting final contestants, we will give heavy preference to companies that launch for the first time to the public and press at FireHack. This usually means releasing your product for the first time (could be into private beta)."
    3. The competition will take place in Mumbai but teams from across the India are welcome to submit their startups for consideration.
    4. Startups that have presented at other public launch events are NOT eligible for FireHack. If you're choosing between launch platforms and need an early decision, please apply and email us at firehack@weekendventures.org and we'll priority review your application.

    Thanks for considering FireHack as your launch platform.

    Frequent Questions about the FireHack

    FAQ

    • What are you looking for in an application?

      The application is required so that we have an opportunity to understand your product. Give great importance to clarity of thought. Write the details in very simple language. The important thing is that you must mention what is innovative, novel in your product.
      Pro tip, we love it when you can walk us through a use case.

    • Is my application confidential?

      All submissions are confidential unless otherwise permitted by applicants.

    • When will finalists be notified?

      We generally take 10 days from the close of submissions to select and notify our finalists. So for example, if applications close July 20th, you can expect to be notified of our decision (either way) by July 30th.

    • Does it cost money to participate?

      Yes. Firehack charges teams to participate. The charges are used for your meals, coffee, energy drinks during the entire event. You also get FireHack swag with your tickets.
      Note: IIT Bombay teams can collect their Passes from the Electronics Club or WNCC.

    • What is included/required when you participate in the FireHack?

      Firehack does not provide any hardware material. However, if your team is sponsored by any of our partner, you will receive any of the following development board only for the event, you will have to return them back at the end of the event.
      Platforms: Arduino, Raspberry Pi, TI launchPad
      In either cases, all teams are required to bring the peripheral sensors & actuators as per their requirements.
